Chicken Adobo

Chicken adobo is a classic Filipino dish made by braising chicken in a savory, tangy, and slightly sweet sauce of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices.

Known for its rich flavor and tender meat, chicken adobo is simmered until the sauce is deeply infused and slightly reduced. Itâ€™s often served with steamed rice and can be customized with ingredients like bay leaves, black pepper, and a touch of sugar.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Bay leaves
  • Peppercorn

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken: Combine chicken, soy sauce, garlic, and pepper in a bowl. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Sear the chicken: In a pot, heat oil and brown the chicken pieces on both sides.
  3. Simmer with sauce: Add vinegar, bay leaves, water, and any leftover marinade. Bring to a boil, then simmer until the chicken is tender.
  4. Reduce and serve: Let the sauce reduce slightly to thicken. Adjust seasoning if needed, then serve hot with rice.
