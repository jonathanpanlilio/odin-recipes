Picadillo

Filipino picadillo is a hearty, savory dish made with ground beef simmered in a tomato-based sauce with potatoes, carrots, and sometimes raisins or peas. The mix of sweet and savory flavors, along with tender vegetables, makes it a comforting meal often served over rice or with pan de sal. It’s a simple yet flavorful staple that brings the warmth of home-cooked Filipino food to the table.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Tomato sauce
  • Potatoes
  • Seasonings: Soy sauce, salt, and pepper

Instructions

  1. Sautè aromatics: Heat oil in a pan and sauté chopped garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Cook the beef: Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat if needed.
  3. Stir sauce and add veggies: Stir in tomato sauce, diced potatoes, and carrots. Let it simmer.
  4. Season: Add soy sauce, salt, pepper, and optional raisins or green peas for extra flavor.
  5. Simmer until tender: Cover and cook until vegetables are soft and the sauce is slightly reduced. Serve hot over rice.
